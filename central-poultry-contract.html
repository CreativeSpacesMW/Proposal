1.

Scope of Work

The Developers agree to provide a comprehensive digital overhaul, including:

  • Full Website Development: A modern, responsive platform hosted on high-performance infrastructure.
  • Interactive Logistical Mapping: GPS-enabled map featuring farms, warehouses, and authorized distributors.
  • Direct Ordering System: A digital portal for direct-to-consumer and B2B order inquiries.
  • Brand Digital Redesign: Updating logos and color palettes for optimized online presence.
  • Local SEO: Optimization for high visibility within Malawian search results.
  • Rollout Strategy: Delivery of a formal marketing and launch plan.
2.

Financial Terms

2.1 Total Project Fee

The Client agrees to pay a total sum of:

MWK 1,800,000.00

(One Million Eight Hundred Thousand Kwacha)

2.2 Payment Schedule

  • Deposit: 50% (MWK 900,000.00) due upon signing this agreement.
  • Final Balance: 50% (MWK 900,000.00) due exactly 30 days after the website deployment (the "Live Date").
3.

Maintenance & Support

3.1 Consultancy

One (1) year of free digital consultancy starting from the Live Date.

3.2 Feature Updates

Five (5) free updates (minor features or technical changes) to be used within three (3) years.

3.3 Training

Formal technical handover and training for the Client's I.T. department.

4.

Client Responsibilities

The following costs and resources are to be provided/covered by the Client and are not included in the MWK 1,800,000 development fee:

4.1 Infrastructure

All costs related to Domain Registration and Web Hosting subscriptions.

4.2 Logistics

All transport costs related to site visits, farm visits, or physical meetings.

4.3 Content

Delivery of all raw data (distributor lists, pricing, and internal documentation).

4.4 Media Production

The Client shall be responsible for all costs associated with photography and videography, including the provision of cameras, equipment rental, and production-related expenses required for the website's visual content.

5.

Intellectual Property

Upon final payment, ownership of the website's visual design and content shall transfer to the Client. The Developers retain the right to showcase the project in professional portfolios.

6.

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all trade secrets, customer data, and internal business logistics strictly confidential.

7.

Termination & Delays

  • If the Client terminates the project before completion, the Deposit remains non-refundable.
  • Delays caused by the late provision of content or media from the Client may extend the project timeline.
8.

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the Laws of Malawi.
